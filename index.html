A short story of Svasthya.

We form one of the premier clinical laboratories and have introduced many esoteric testing services that are otherwise sourced out of the state. Having solved that problem of getting the “testing samples” to commute large distances by introducing testing facilities for many parameters in our state, we strive to help physicians get quicker diagnostic support in treatment and therapeutics.
